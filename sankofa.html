SANKOFA COMMUNITY GARDEN
Each student developed a building proposal for a new center for Urban Agriculture in Pittsburgh. The site was speci cally Sankofa Community Garden located in Homewood. The center will serve as a hub supporting urban gardening and farming activity in Pittsburgh. The center will also become an outreach center allowing for education and community engagement from the citizens of Homewood.
