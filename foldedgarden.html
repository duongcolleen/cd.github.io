Folded Garden is a project focused on constructing a growing armature to sustain a plant through the season’s rst frost. Students were given the opportunity to explore di erent shapes and plant properties that they wanted to take into account when constructing an armature for their given plant. The materials that were given included 0.062” or 0.032” thick wire and heat shrink wrap.
