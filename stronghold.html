FIRST Stronghold (2016)
Team Kika Mana #368's Robot
Objective: Breaching opponents' defenses and capturing their tower by firing "boulders" (small foam balls) at it and surrounding/scaling the tower.
Robot: This robot's intake uses a set of rollers that allows the boulder to be intaken from any sides of the robot (right, left, straight-forward). The shooter can be controlled to turn to any angle to allow for the driver to be able to specifically angle their shot to make the high tower target.
