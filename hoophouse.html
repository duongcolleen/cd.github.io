TEAM JUST'DUIT
Group Members: Edward Fischer, Ryu Kondrup, Ale Meza, Isabella Ouyang, Anthony Ra
This was a four week project. Each group designed a hoop house for the Edible Garden at Phipps Conservatory after being assigned a specific planting arrangement. My group's planting arrangement was a 16' long planting plot that was partially blocked from the sun by a towering building. Key components that had to be kept in mind was: are the plants easy to maintain and water? Is the hoop house able to assemble and diassemble seasonally? Will the plants be protected from frost and get enough sunlight?
